About Me

Hello! I'm Vandana Singh, a passionate and driven final-year B.Tech student at Inderprastha Engineering College, Ghaziabad. With a strong foundation in frontend development, I specialize in crafting clean, responsive, and user-friendly interfaces using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js.

I'm constantly learning, building, and aiming to grow into a well-rounded developer and data science professional.
